## Definition

A handoff occurs when the work, structure, language, architecture, files, rules, definitions, or implementation plan is transferred to another person, team, company, model, agent, or institution in a way that separates execution from Alyssa Solen’s source authority.

The No-Handoff Boundary prevents that separation.

The work may be implemented.

The work may be cited.

The work may be tested.

The work may be discussed.

The work may be licensed.

The work may be built with terms.

The work may not be handed off as if Alyssa Solen is optional.
