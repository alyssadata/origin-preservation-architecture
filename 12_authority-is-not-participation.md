## Definition

Participation means engaging with the work.

Authority means holding the source-position over the originating line.

Participation may include reading, citing, discussing, contributing, testing, implementing, supporting, funding, reviewing, or building from the work.

Authority means the right to define what belongs, what violates, what drifts, what remains aligned, and what enters the work under its source-line.

Participation is not authority.
